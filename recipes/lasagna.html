Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical
lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients,
such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Make the Meat Sauce
  2. Cook the Noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
